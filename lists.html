Hot Cocoa Recipe

Ingredients

  • Two Tables spoons of Wired Brain cocoa mix
  • 8 oz of milk
  • Marshmallows {optionl}

Steps

  1. Scoop hot cocoa mix into a mug
  2. Heat milk in stove or microwave below boil
  3. Pour milk into a jag, stirring gently
  4. Stir untill fully dissolved
  5. Top with Marshmellow
